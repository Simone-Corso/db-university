
Selezionare tutti gli studenti nati nel 1990:   

SELECT `id`, `degree_id`, `name`, `surname`, `date_of_birth`, `fiscal_code`, `enrolment_date`, `registration_number`, `email` 
FROM `students` 
WHERE YEAR(`date_of_birth`) = 1990;
